Introduction To Dogs

Note: This topic is a placeholder and will likely be changed later down the line.

Many people will say that dogs are man's best friend. But where did this saying first originate? Research suggests that the saying comes from the oldest days of hunter-gatherers, where man worked with wolves to find food and survive.

In fact, wolves (and therefore dogs) existed millions of years before humans roamed the earth, though not exactly as we know them.

Reasons why dog owners are happy:

  • Companionship
  • Interactivity
  • Many unique shapes, colors, and sizes
  • They're just so cute
